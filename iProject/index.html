Welcome to Whooville Heights!

Thank you for visiting the official website of Whoville Heights Property Owners' Association (WHPOA).

Whooville Heights is an enchanting community located in Mt. Crumpit Whooville. Getting to and from within our magical community is a breeze. Nestled in a picturesque valley surrounded by rolling hills and lush forests, Whoville is a place where creativity and cheerfulness thrive. The town is famous for its charming, candy-colored houses and winding streets that seem to dance through the landscape, reflecting the unique personalities of those that call this place home. The lively architecture and colorful gardens create a sense of wonder, making Whoville a delightful place to visit and explore. Whoville Heights is a favorite and highly desired community!

If you like being outdoors but not far from home, you will love the town square, a gathering place where locals and visitors come together to celebrate. The square is home to a variety of shops and eateries, each with its own quirky style, offering everything from delicious baked goods to one-of-a-kind handmade crafts. At the center stands a towering clock tower, which not only marks the hours but also serves as a reminder of the town's traditions and history. Whoville's residents are known for their infectious energy and warm-heartedness, always eager to spread cheer and make visitors feel at home.

Nearby shopping includes The Shops at Eastville, Whovilles' Premium Outlets, The Grinch-Grove and much more. There is no shortage of restaurants either, whatever you crave, your appetite will be satisfied. Whoville is also renowned for its seasonal celebrations, especially the holiday season, when the entire town is transformed into a winter wonderland. During this time, homes and storefronts are adorned with twinkling lights and festive decorations, while the air is filled with the sound of carolers and laughter. The annual Whoville Christmas celebration is a cherished tradition, featuring parades, feasts, and the iconic tree-lighting ceremony. The spirit of togetherness is at its peak, as Whoville embodies the true meaning of joy, community, and holiday magic as they celebrate Christmas.

Whoville Heights students attend highly rated Mt. Crumpit Schools including our very own Whoville Elementary School easily walkable for many residents. Nearby charter and private schools offer educational alternatives. Higher education opportunities include Whoville University located in Whoville and nearby University of South Whoville, Grinch-Grove University, and the University of Whoville.

Enjoy your time in Whoville!
